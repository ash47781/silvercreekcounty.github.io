Doctor Who is about The Doctor who Travels in time and space

Doctor Who is a time travel show based around a single character named The Doctor. Nobody knows The Doctors real name but he is an inspiration to many people around the world.
The Doctor recruits companions to travel through time and space with him because he gets lonely and wants to explore new things with others.

The Doctor is an alien and their species is called Time Lords. Time Lords come from a planet called Gallifrey
