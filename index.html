Welcome to Silver Creek County!!

Huxley and Mac made Silver Creek County to get away from all of the toxicity and favoritism
in other counties so we made our own and welcome everyone in to have fun in a county where
everything is fair and no one is mistreated in the county. We hope you enjoy your time
here and keep coming back to have fun in the county so we can expand
and grow so we can have fun with more and more people.
