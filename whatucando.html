Season 1:
E1: Rose - This is the first episode of the first season of the 2005 series where The Doctor meets Rose and invites here to be a companion through space and time travel.
E2: The End of the World - This episode is about The Doctor and Rose going on their first adventure to the end of the Earth where The Doctor saves the Space Station their on.
E3: The Unquiet Dead - This episode is during Charles Dickens times and there is "ghosts" that want to kill the humans but The Doctor stops them at a terrible cost.
E4: Aliens of London - This episode is about how the Prime Minister of London has disappeared and no one can find him and the acting prime minister is an alien. This is Part 1/2.
E5: World War Three - This is the 2nd part of the previous episode where the aliens almost destroy London with nukes using a lie saying theres enemy alien ships in orbit.
