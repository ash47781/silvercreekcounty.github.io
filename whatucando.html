The opportunities of Silver Creek County!

In Silver Creek County we offer multiple different job opportunities and some jobs that you can recieve some spare money. We offer different jobs that can
craft different items based on the jobs title and can do different things based off of the job like a train conductor can drive trains but
a blacksmith can make things for a conductor to use. We also offer wagons and horse missions that specific missions can use
different kinds of wagons or horses to do different missions in example a log delivery requires a log wagon or a coal wagon requires a coal wagon.
We want everyone to have something to do in the county so they feel like they belong.
